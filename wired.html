What is a Wired Network?

A wired network is a type of network that uses physical cables to connect devices and transmit data. They use cables such as Ethernet cables, Fiber Optics, etc, which connect devices together.

What is the Hardware needed for Wired Networks?

  • Ethernet Cables - The physical connection between devices on the network.
  • Network Adapters - Built into devices and allow them to communicate over the network.
  • Router - Acts as a gateway for the network, connecting it to the internet and allowing devices to communicate.
  • Switch - Connects multiple devices within a network, allowing them to communicate directly with each other.
  • Modem - Converts signals from your internet service provider to your router, connecting you to the internet.
