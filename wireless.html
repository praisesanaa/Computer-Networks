What is a Wireless Network?

A wireless network is a networks that send data through the air using radio waves. Wireless networks use specific radio frequency ranges that are regulated by the Federal Communications Commission (FCC).


  

What is the Hardware needed for Wireless Networks?

  • Modem - Converts signals from your internet service provider to your router, connecting you to the internet.
  • Wireless Router - Broadcasts the wireless signal, acting as the central hub for your network.
  • Wireless Network Adapters - Allow devices like laptops, tablets, and smartphones to connect to the wireless network.
  • Antennas - Transmit and receive wireless signals.
  • Ethernet Cables - Connect the modem and router, and can also be used for wired connections.
  • Switches - Can be used to create multiple wired connections on the network.
